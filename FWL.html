Sunday 18th March

Time to self-isolate

Today is the last day (for now) that I will be working in the office, we have been told to now work from home

It is a new experience, once seen to be a great thing to do, but now, for me, quite lonely.

It is nice to know that I can spend more time at home with Lara and Luna, have lunch with them and be able to just come downstairs when finished for the day. Whilst we shall be saving money with not having to drive anywhere, we shall possibly see that increase in electric.

I have now stopped teaching my exercise classes for now, which means that I won't have any reason to leave the house. My best intention is to ensure that I am walking on my lunch hour so that I get fresh air and exercise.
