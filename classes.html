CLASSES

Dance&Tone

Mondays 6.30pm-7.15pm

DANCE&TONE is a dance based cardio exercise class designed to help you burn calories, tone up and have fun at the same time! A great class to start your fitness journey with or to add some fun into your current regime. We twerk, shimmy, mambo and drop (just to name a few!) to a variety of music styles creating an easy to follow yet challenging workout.

This is followed by 15 minutes of legs bums and tummy toning to finish the class off. Suitable for all abilities and fitness levels as well as anyone aged 16+ (14+ if accomanied by an adult)

£5 per class

Stretch&Tone

Mondays 7.15pm-8.00pm

STRETCH&TONE is a low impact class designed to suit most abilities. We start the class by warming up using some flowing movements based on sun salutations and yoga followed by a low impact yet results driven full body toning section. This will then be finished with a full body stretch and a relaxation/mindfulness section.

Designed to be hard work, without making it high impact it is the perfect class to start off with or to add to your schedule if you are a keen exerciser. The toning will shape, tone and tighten your body whereas the stretching will relieve your muscles helping to prevent injuries and with general well-being.

£4 per class or do DANCE&TONE or HIIT & STRETCH&TONE for £6!

Dan's BOOTCAMP

Thursdays 6.30pm-7.00pm

HIIT is high intensity interval training, designed to push your body to get the results you strive for. Be prepared to work hard for 30 minutes but it will be worth it! Fat loss, calorie burning, muscle building and sculpting are just a few of the benefits from taking part in this class. This class also keeps burning calories for up to 24 hours after participating which makes it a great addition to your fitness regime. There are modifications throughout to help you build up to working to your maximum level making it suitable for everyone who wants to challenge themselves and increase their fitness levels. Must be 16+ to attend.
